
安装apk，然后到设置那边允许apk的所有权限。

先连上耳机，然后打开app，点击CONNECT SCO，连上SCO后，按钮会变为DISCONNECT SCO，


然后点击播放按钮，就会往蓝牙耳机播放声音; 点击录音按钮，会开始从蓝牙耳机录音，保存为/sdcard/n.amr，


如果要播放蓝牙a2dp音乐时候，要先点击DISCONNECT SCO来断开sco，这样蓝牙a2dp音乐a2dp才能正常播放。

install scoaudiotest.apk,and then go to Settings to allow all the permissions for scoaudiotest.apk.

connect to BT headset first,and then open this app, click ‘CONNECT SCO’，if connect sucess, the button switch to ‘DISCONNECT SCO'.

after connect to sco, click ‘play’,app will play sound to BT headset; 
click ‘record’, app will record sound from BT headset,and save to  /sdcard/n.amr
